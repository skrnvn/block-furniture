how we do it?

The aim of this project is to create and bring to market an exclusive range of high-end small to medium-sized pieces of furniture to open up and demonstrate new creative possibilities for our material through a collaborative partnership with Collective HQ.

The table tops will be handmade at our dedicated plant in Preston, Lancashire. Each will be unique, because of the natural differences inherent to handmaking a product. The table bases have been designed to use waste and offcuts from local manufacturers who use Forest Stewardship Council (FSC) sourced plywood.

get closer to sustainable development with us!

  • Reusability and recyclability

    Our tables are actually made almost entirely from waste that would be otherwise heading for landfill. So not only do we not put anything in landfill, our products actually divert significant volumes of material away from landfill. All raw materials are inert, so do not perish or degrade whilst in storage.

  • Sustainable Distribution

    Fundamental to the entire philosophy of Alusid is environmentally responsible manufacturing, utilising recycled waste and energy-efficient production methods. Hence, sustainability is a core value and attribute within both the company and all our product ranges.

  • Sustainable materials

    As all Alusid's products are made from no less than 98% recycled waste, by their very nature they are low-impact materials. Combined with energy-efficient manufacturing, our entire product range is low in environmental impacts.
